BETR Token ICO Process

BETR tokens will be available for purchase by following the link on the website. Sales begin to the public at 14:00 GMT on Friday 15th December 2017.

Register Now

Stage 1: Something

Purchasers will need to register on the website and confirm their email address prior to purchasing. They will then be able to select the currency that they wish to use to purchase tokens and will be assigned a unique wallet address in that currency to send funds to. All incoming funds will be displayed in the customer dashboard together with an indicator as to how far they have progressed in the process of blockchain confirmation. Funds may be remitted in BTC, BCH, ETH, LTC and DASH. Funds may also be received in FIAT (US$) – these payments are limited to $10K or more.

Stage 2: Something

Once the incoming funds are cleared customers will be able to claim tokens by clicking the “claim tokens” button next to the transaction. All customers will be required to submit proof of identity and address (KYC) prior to their tokens being issued. This is a very simple process involves uploading on the website two images with the relevant documentation.

Stage 3: Something

Once the KYC documents have been confirmed by our team the account will be activated and customers will be asked to supply an ethereum address to receive the tokens. Tokens will then be sent to this address.

Stage 4: Something

This entire process is handled in our ICO software and very easy to use. We also present a link within the software that includes a referral code so that customers who wish to earn bounty (5%) by referring people to the ICO can have that referral tracked and the bounty paid on any purchases.

Stage 5: Something

Tokens are issued according to the exchange rate as per coinmarketcap.com at the time that the incoming payment is received. Purchasers will be able to see in the customer dashboard how many tokens will be issued for any purchase directly after the funds are received.
