Guide to Buying BETR

Thanks for your interest in the BETR token. So you want to buy BETR tokens? That’s great! We have prepared a step-by-step guide for you. In case of any questions, we’re glad to help you in our live chat on Telegram or Twitter or email us here.

Joining the Token sale is done in 5 steps:

  1. Account registration / Email verification
  2. Choose from BTC, ETH, BCH, LTC, DASH to send your funds
  3. Send funds to the unique wallet created for you
  4. Funds clear through blockchain and then you can claim tokens
  5. Complete ID (KYC) check
  6. Tokens are Issued

We’ll go through these steps and explain the details. It’s all quite simple and quick so let’s begin

Stage 1: Account registration

  • Go to https://betterbetting.org/ico-sales.html and click ‘Register’
  • Enter your Email and choose a password (Upper and lower case, number and symbol)
  • Verify your email through the link we send you
  • You can also register directly using social media logins from Facebook or twitter (don’t add bit we will add later)

Stage 2: Choose your tokens

  • Choose your payment cryptocurrency
  • Enter the amount you want to purchase – your bonus tokens will be added automatically

Stage 3: Send Funds

  • You will be assigned your personal unique wallet address in your chosen currency for purchasing tokens
  • Go to your wallet and send funds to this unique address. Please make sure your deposit equals or exceeds the minimum purchase amount of $100
  • All incoming funds will be displayed on your dashboard together with an indicator as to how far they have progressed in the process of blockchain confirmation.
  • Funds may be remitted in BTC, BCH, ETH, LTC and DASH. Funds may also be received in FIAT (US$) – these payments are limited to $10K or more at this stage.
  • Funds will appear in your BETR account when the transaction gets a certain amount of confirmations from network. This can take from 10 minutes to 2 hours

Stage 4: Claim tokens and ID Check

  • Once the incoming funds are cleared, you will be able to claim tokens by clicking the “claim tokens” button next to the transaction

Stage 5: Complete ID (KYC) check

  • You will be required to submit proof of identity and address (KYC) prior to their tokens being issued. This is a very simple process which involves uploading two images on the website with the relevant documentation. We will inform you once this is approved.

Stage 6: Tokens issued

  • Once the KYC documents have been confirmed by our team your account will be activated and you will be asked to supply an ethereum address to receive the tokens.
  • Tokens will then be sent to this address and you will own your BETR tokens!
